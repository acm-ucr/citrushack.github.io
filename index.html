What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a place where the only thing limiting what you build is own creativity.

What should I create?

You can build whatever you want: websites, apps, or hardware (no soldering allowed)

Who can come?

Undergraduate or graduate students. We may also admit high school students but please notify us.

Do I need to have a team?

Nope! Just come by at the start of the event and you'll find tons of great people to form a team with.

What other rules should I be aware of?

We're glad you asked! Alcohol is forbidden. Also, no soldering irons, open flames, or anything.

How are hacks presented?

On Sunday at 12:00pm, all hackers will be able to demo their hacks to sponsors and their peers.
